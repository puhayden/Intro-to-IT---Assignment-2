  • Team Member: Chalani

    Working with team members of THURS Group #25 has been very interesting and enjoyable. In fact, collaborating with team members who have various cultural backgrounds, personalities, ideas and skills has been very useful to successfully achieve Assignment 3 goals. As a group, we have been able to improve many aspects of teamwork compared to the previous Assignment 2. As a matter of fact, we have been able to early plan all the meetings ahead, created an excel sheet to keep a track of the progress of each team member's assigned work, and above all we have been able to respect all the soft deadlines. Although we were able to improve our capabilities of teamwork over the course of the group assignments, if there is anything that we could get further improved, that would be our team coordination skills. One thing that I have learned while working in a group is that planning ahead and everyone’s equal contribution to the project will lead us to complete and deliver a quality project on time with peace of mind. One surprising thing that I have noticed in each team member are as follows: Samantha’s ability to efficiently lead weekly group meetings on Teams, Liam’s impeccable attention to detail, Alexander’s knowledge in Information Technology field, Matthew’s ability to create extraordinary excel spreadsheets to track the progress and interface designing and last but not least Hayden’s energy and the capability to style and make attractive HTML pages. Overall, as a team we are happy about our team and the deliveries.
