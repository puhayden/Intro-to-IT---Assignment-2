Group Processes

The THURS Group # 25 for Introduction to Information Technology has 6 team members, and they are: Samantha, Chalani, Hayden, Liam, Alexander and Matthew.

In Assignment 2, the group had some difficulties at the beginning of the assignment in organising various group meetings through Microsoft Teams due to poor communication among team members and the lack of a proper meeting plan. However, the cohesion of the team members and their willingness to achieve good grades in Assignment 2 have encouraged everyone to take immediate actions and to set up proper meeting dates twice per week even if it was a bit behind time. Firstly, after brainstorming ideas we have decided to split and allocate various parts of the assignment to each team member taking into consideration their capabilities and passion towards certain topics specified in the assignment specifications. Secondly, we have agreed to set up a few soft deadlines to edit, revise and to format report content with the assumption to finalise various project areas prior to the hard deadline. The main goal of the soft deadlines was to finalise the writing contents of the report in order to have a considerable amount of time to dedicate on GitHub, HTML coding and CSS styling before the assignment due date. Furthermore, weekly organised group meetings helped all the team members to streamline and support each other whenever they encountered any difficulties in allocated sections, or any other issue related to the technical side of GitHub as well as with HTML coding. Also, the usage of communication platforms like Microsoft Teams and the usage of Google Docs not only facilitated us to monitor the progress updates of each member but also to provide feedback to each other’s work and to verify whether the assigned portion of work adhered to the assignment rubric specifications to obtain full marks. Additionally, keeping a track of GitHub log activity has been very useful to keep an eye on the progress of HTML codes and CSS styling of the project. Moreover, working with team members who have different personalities, capabilities, strengths and skills have been very helpful for knowledge sharing as well as to achieve desired goals. In fact, most of the personality tests result outcomes gained for each team member in Assignment 1 revealed to be in line with the behaviours and attitudes that they have demonstrated during the Assignment 2. For instance, Samantha’s energy, motivation and punctuality helped the entire team to have well organised team meetings with excellent outcomes. Chalani’s organising skills and planning ahead not only demonstrated her good leadership qualities but also her capability to make sure that no one missed their work, and they are on track have been much appreciated. Hayden on the other hand with his calm yet observant character, assisted the team by putting up a mind-blowing web page to host all the report content as well as to offer help to team members struggling with HTML coding and styling. Moreover, Alexander’s flexibility to adapt to any situation as per the requirement has been a great advantage to overcome obstacles came with the Assignment 2. Also, his previously acquired in-depth knowledge in various IT related subjects has been a plus point while covering different project tasks. In addition, Liam’s innovative project ideas along with his positive attitude in handling any difficult/unpredictable situation within the group has ensured a smooth and efficient workflow. Finally, Matthew not only has been a very good team member who continuously looked after any team member in need of assistance but also made sure to create a dedicated Microsoft Excel sheet to track the progress of each team member’s work. The spreadsheet created by Matthew enabled other team members to tick off their finalised work gradually and this in turn updated a pie chart which represented the progress percentage of the whole project.

However, reflecting back on Assignment 2, we have to admit that there have been few difficulties and unpredictable situations that we had to face in the course of the project work. To begin with, the group had difficulties in gathering all group members together during Teams meetings. In fact, lack of pre planning all the upcoming meeting dates and times affected most of the soft deadlines as well as created stressful situations during the project. Also, a group member was unable to deliver the assigned portion of work on time before the Assignment deadline due to lack of communication and responsibility. It has not only created a stressful situation among remaining group members but also resulted in forcing other team members to deliver the project report with a missing part. However, this team member apologised for the inconvenience caused and mentioned that he will not let the same to happen with future projects.

Despite the difficulties faced during Assignment 2, all team members agree on the fact that working on group projects have enhanced our individual capabilities and it has benefited our teamwork skills. Also, the cohesion of the team and the well understanding of each other’s personalities, strengths and weaknesses led us to successfully complete the assigned tasks and to acknowledge the areas to be improved when approaching future group work.

Finally, there are a number of changes that we will be introducing for Assignment 3 in order to achieve better grades in the future. For instance, we are looking forward to planning and setting up dates and times for team meetings in the beginning of the Assignment 3 which will let us work with peace of mind and carry out the deliveries while respecting all the soft deadlines for the completion of specific tasks. There will be 2 meetings per week on Teams and each group member is expected to be present with a functioning web camera and microphone. In case a team member is unable to attend a meeting, they are expected to re-watch the recorded meeting on Teams or to ask another team member to summarise what has been discussed during the meeting. Moreover, we will strictly adhere and respect soft deadlines to achieve meaningful outcomes and to avoid last minute pressure. Lastly, we will make sure to use all the available communication platforms effectively and efficiently to maintain good and harmonious communication among team members.
