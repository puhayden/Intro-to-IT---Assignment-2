Also, the perfect UX/UI designer will be responsible for delivering the best user experience to ensure maximum customer satisfaction with improved usability and visual designs.

    • In-depth knowledge of User Experience methodologies and design thinking.
    • Ability to design attractive and functional mobile interfaces.
    • Sound knowledge in design software tools like Sketch, Figma, Balsamiq, Photoshop and Axure.
    • Proven experience with Swift UI.
    • Proven knowledge in identifying and troubleshooting UX/UI design problems.
    • Ability to understand business requirements and create user-centred designs accordingly.
    • Experience in conducting qualitative and quantitative research related to UX/UI designs.
    • Ability to create solid designs after attentively analysing UX/UI research insights.
    • Experience in creating attractive wireframes, prototypes and storyboards.
    • Proven experience in working with IOS platforms.
    • Ability to clearly communicate about designs with precision and clarity.
    • In-depth knowledge in native mobile technologies.
    • Ability to timely deliver UX/UI mock-ups and prototypes.
    • Capability to adhere to UX/UI platform conventions and style standards
    • Proven experience in working as part of the development team.
    • Ability to make quick and effective decisions.
    • Excellent problem solving skills.
    • Strong attention to detail.
    • Excellent communication skills.
    • Ability to prioritize tasks.
    • Excellent time management skills.
